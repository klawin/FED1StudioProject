Class Subjects


Pre-Algebra

Pre Algebra introduces students to subjects like integers, one-step equations, inequalities, percents, fractions, and more.

Algebra I

Algebra 1 covers notation, multi-step equations, linear and exponential equations, linear inequalities, linear and exponential functions, quadratic functions, fractional equations, polynomials and more.

Geometry

Geometry is the study of sizes, shapes, positions, angles and dimensions of objects on a plane. In this course we cover calculation of area, perimeter, sine, cosine, tangent and more.

Algebra II

Algebra II covers polynomial arithmetic, complex numbers, polynomial factorization, polynomial division, and so much more.

Pre-Calculus

Pre-Calulus is the combination of trigonometry and math analysis. It bridges the gap to Calculus as you learn references to things like the unit circle, composite functions and other concepts necessary for you to move forward.

Calculus

Calculus is the mathematical study of continuous change. In this course you will learn about Limits, Derivatives, and last but certainly not least, Integrals.
